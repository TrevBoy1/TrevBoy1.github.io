Wausau High School Unification Under Controversy

Wausau's plans to merge its eastern and western high schools continue to stir local controversies

The moderately populated city of Wausau has had continuous plans to merge the city's titular eastern and western high schools into a singular entity. No major changes are underway, and it is becoming more likely that the situation will remain this way. The Wausau School Board declared a 5-4 vote against the merger.


Pat McKee has been an advocate for the merger, however, has been accepting of the general opinion surrounding it.
